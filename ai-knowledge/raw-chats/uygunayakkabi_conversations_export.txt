7. WEBSITE PURPOSE (BUSINESS MODEL)
-------------------------------------------------
The site is NOT intended to be a traditional e‑commerce checkout.

Instead:

Customer Flow

User visits site
        ↓
Browses shoes
        ↓
Clicks buy
        ↓
Leaves name + phone number
        ↓
Seller calls customer
        ↓
Sale completed manually

Advantages:

• simpler logistics
• no payment integration required
• faster launch
• lower risk
